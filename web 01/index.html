Founder of Amazon

"Jeffrey Preston Bezos" (/ˈbeɪzoʊs/;[a][2] né Jorgensen; born January 12, 1964)[3] is an American internet entrepreneur, industrialist, media proprietor, and investor. He is best known as the founder, CEO, and president of the multi-national technology company Amazon. The first centi-billionaire on the Forbes wealth index,[4] Bezos has been the world's richest person since 2017 and was named the "richest man in modern history" after his net worth increased to $150 billion in July 2018.[5] According to Forbes, he is the first person in history to have a net worth exceeding $200 billion.[6]
